## CRITICAL ISSUES (Must Fix)

### 1. Question #874 - Canada Day "Always" Celebrated with Fireworks
**Question**: "Canada Day is always celebrated with fireworks and outdoor festivities."  
**Current Answer**: True  
**Issue**: The word "always" makes this factually incorrect. Not every location celebrates with fireworks (remote areas, weather issues, budget constraints, private celebrations, etc.)

**Recommendation**: 
- Change answer to **False**, OR
- Rework question: "Canada Day is typically celebrated with fireworks and outdoor festivities." (True)

### 2. Question #891 - Party Leaders "Always" Elected
**Question**: "Political party leaders in Canada are always elected by party members."  
**Current Answer**: True  
**Issue**: Not always true - interim leaders can be appointed, and smaller parties may use different selection methods.

**Recommendation**:
- Change answer to **False**, OR
- Rework question: "Political party leaders in Canada are typically elected by party members." (True)
